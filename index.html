Tempo and Beat Annotation

  • Space to play/pause
  • Left click to move the playhead
  • Scroll to... scroll
  • Right click to place a beat
  • Right click it again to delete it
  • Ctrl+Right click to mark a tempo change
  • Placing two beats will calculate the tempo and show where following beats will land according to that tempo.
  • If the tempo changes at some point, mark it by placing a tempo change to end the previous constant tempo region.
